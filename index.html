Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 09-08-2024

At CitrusEye, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from our users. The CitrusEye app allows farmers to capture images and get results, but no user data, images, or any other form of information is collected or stored by the app.

2. Use of Camera

CitrusEye requires access to your device's camera for the sole purpose of capturing images of orange trees to provide you with a summary of results. These images are processed on your device and are not transmitted or stored by CitrusEye.

3. No Data Storage

We do not collect or store any personal information, images, or other data. All processing is done locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

CitrusEye does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and become effective immediately upon posting.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at admin@citruseye.com.
